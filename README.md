I'm an Economics graduate from the University of Waterloo with research interests in Development Economics, Innovation Economics, and International Trade. Currently working as an Economic Affairs Intern at UNESCAP researching AI adoption in trade facilitation.

## Research Projects 

### Senior Honours Thesis
**AI Diffusion and Productivity Divergence in Developing Economies** (2025)
- Investigated whether AI technology promotes economic convergence or exacerbates inequality through international trade channels
- Analyzed panel data across 47 countries from 2012-2022
- Developed novel concordance methodology linking bilateral trade data to AI exposure estimates
- Supervised by Professor Horatiu Rus
